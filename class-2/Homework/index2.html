Who are we?

Orchestra is a fun and creative way to start your music career!
We come in many sizes ,Violins, Violas, and Cellos.
These three main instruments make up the most of the orchestra.
Lets dive deeper a see that this magical place is all about!





Violins

The smallest but most used out of all three.
With this instrument, you can play the highest
note of them all (E string)
Violins makes up about 2/5th of an orchestra.
We play the main part of the song, called the melody.

Violas

Violas are the second largest instrument out of the string family.
they have a mid tone, making them very versatile.
this means that they can play high and low notes. They pair well with the cello as
the melody. Many people have a hard time seeing the difference
between the violin and the violas.

Cellos

Last up, the big Cellos. Cellos are the biggest out of all three instruments
they have a rich deep sound whom adds that extra depth into a song.
They are so big that the user must lay the cello over there body to use. (not holding up)
They usually get the background music. People think thats lame but a matter of fact,
its very important.
